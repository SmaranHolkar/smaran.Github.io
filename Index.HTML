How much maths does computer science need?

 It really depends on what type programming or work you are doing with computers, if you are making a game then you will need maths for the physics that the game will use like suspension in cars, but the maths for machine learning and AI (artificial inteligence) will be much different. In general Calculus, algebra, discrete mathematics, and statistics are all required in most degree programmes. All degrees related to maths will need you to have a good knowledge in different type of maths needed.
